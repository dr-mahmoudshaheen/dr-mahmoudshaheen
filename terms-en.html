Terms and conditions governing the services of Dr. Mahmoud Shaheen Law Office

1. Definitions

In these terms and conditions:

  • The Office: refers to Dr. Mahmoud Shaheen Law Office

  • Services: include all legal services provided by the Office
  • Agreement: the contract concluded between the Office and the Client

2. Scope of Services

The Office provides the following legal services:

  • Legal consultations in all branches of law
  • Legal representation before Egyptian courts
  • Drafting contracts and legal memoranda
  • Legal follow-up of cases
  • Mediation and arbitration

3. Office Obligations

4. Client Obligations

The Office commits to maintaining confidentiality of all client and case-related information, and may not disclose it except with written client consent or by order of competent judicial authority.

8. Liability

The Office commits to exercising required professional care, and is not liable for final case outcomes except in cases of gross negligence or professional misconduct.
